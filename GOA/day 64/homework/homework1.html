Mercedes Benz

Mercedes was the 1st company to patent the automobile in 1885. Mercedes was light years ahead of the competition and produced the first hybrid car, gasoline and electric, in 1906. This was subsequently dropped in favour of entirely gasoline-fossil fuel-powered vehicles.

First car of Mercedes-benz

Mercedes-Benz's first car was the Benz Patent-Motorwagen, created by Karl Benz in 1886. This vehicle is widely regarded as the first true automobile because it was powered by an internal combustion engine. It had a lightweight design with three wheels and a rear-mounted single-cylinder engine. The car was capable of reaching a top speed of about 16 km/h (10 mph).

Last model of mercedes best benz

As of 2024, one of the latest Mercedes-Benz cars is the Mercedes-Benz EQS. It’s part of the EQ series, which represents the brand's push into the luxury electric vehicle market. The EQS is an all-electric luxury sedan, combining top-tier comfort, cutting-edge technology, and powerful electric performance.

Mercedes-Benz: From the First to the Latest Car

  • Power Source: Motorwagen uses internal combustion engine, EQS is fully electric
  • Design and Comfort: Motorwagen is barebones with no roof or doors, EQS is a luxury sedan with advanced features
  • Speed and Performance: Motorwagen has a top speed of 16 km/h, EQS exceeds 200 km/h
  • Technological Features: Motorwagen has minimal mechanics, EQS has autonomous driving and advanced digital systems
  • Sustainability: Motorwagen relies on fossil fuels, EQS is an emission-free electric vehicle
